About Us

At Young Contractors Ltd, we pride ourselves on being a leading tiling company that specialises in the supply and installation of natural stone, porcelain, large-format tiles, and mosaic wall/floor tiling.

We also offer bespoke stone worktops in the residential, retail, leisure, and commercial sectors. With a commitment to excellence, we offer comprehensive supply and installation services designed to meet the unique needs of each of our clients.

Tiling Solutions

Residential Tiling

Transform your project with our expert tiling services, whether it’s for a new bathroom, kitchen, or any other living space.

Retail Spaces

Create stunning retail environments that attract customers and enhance your brand with our custom tiling solutions.

Leisure Facilities

Enhance the aesthetic appeal and functionality of your leisure spaces with our durable and stylish tiles.

Commercial Properties

Optimise your commercial spaces with our high-quality tiling that combines beauty with practicality. We understand the importance of making our projects as beneficial for our clients as possible, ensuring cost-effectiveness and efficiency.

Wet Room Systems

Using top-of-the-range materials, waterproofing your bathroom correctly is vital to a long-lasting area.

Tiled Pedestal System

Tiled terraces and balconies are an ever-present requirement. We can use our metal frame system or pedestals to create your perfect space.

Kitchen Tops, Splash backs, Vanity Units, Bespoke Items

From templating to installation, we deliver bespoke kitchen tops and splash backs that are both functional and beautiful.

Vanity Tops

Add a touch of elegance to your bathrooms with our custom-made vanity tops, designed to meet your specific preferences.

Repair & Maintenance Services
