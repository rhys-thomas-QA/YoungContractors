Key Design Decisions

  • Nav: Clean horizontal nav with logo left, links right. Contact as a CTA button. Hamburger on mobile.
  • Footer: Dark charcoal with contact info, quick links, certifications (Tile Association, RB Services), and Instagram.
  • Palette: Off white (#fafaf7) backgrounds, warm stone (#f0ede6) accent areas, forest green (#3c5544) CTAs, charcoal (#2d2d2d) text and footer.
  • Typography: Libre Baskerville for headings, system sans-serif for body text.
  • Responsive: Mobile-first with hamburger nav, stacked layouts below 768px.
